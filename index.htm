Nike Tanjun Ladies Trainers Ladies


These Nike Tanjun Trainers feature a meshed upper to offer breathability to your feet. A chunky foam like sole offers cushioning, situated on the upper is a laced up front fastening with a shaped ankle collar with a cushioned insole to provide a secure , position locked fit. A striking Nike Swoosh is situated on the sides of the trainers to complete the overall look.
